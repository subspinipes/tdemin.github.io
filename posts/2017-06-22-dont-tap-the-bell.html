Don't Tap the Bell!

June 22, 2017

Timur Demin

The more I watch YouTube videos, the more I see channels, which owners beg you to tap the bell located close to “Subscribe” button. It is common for them to specify YouTube is laggy, notifications don’t always work, etc.

In a few words — don’t do it. Not only YouTube is operating normally, but this bell enables push notifications for channel notifications. Or, in other words, your phone/tablet connected to your Google account will receive every single notification of channel events as soon as it arrives.

This means your phone will ring every time another man releases a new video. He/she could release it at any time, and your device would notify you even if it’s night, and you’re sleeping tight in your bed. Most people don’t bother with setting up notifications levels and Do not Disturb, so your device will probably ring at full volume. Do you want it? I doubt so.

If you click the bell as the channel owner begs you, you’ll only suffer from that click. Don’t do it.
